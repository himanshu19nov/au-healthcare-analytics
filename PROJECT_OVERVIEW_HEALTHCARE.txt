THE BUSINESS PROBLEM
--------------------
The Australian public hospital system faces systemic pressures across three 
critical domains: Emergency Department (ED) access, Elective Surgery capacity, 
and overall Health Expenditure.

Key challenges include:

1. EMERGENCY DEPARTMENT OVERLOAD
   Presentations are increasing annually, leading to longer waiting times. 
   Identifying which triage categories and hospital peer groups are failing 
   to meet clinical targets (e.g., % seen on time) is critical for policy.

2. ELECTIVE SURGERY BACKLOGS
   Waitlists for non-urgent surgeries have ballooned. There is no unified view 
   comparing state-by-state performance or identifying which specialties (e.g., 
   Ophthalmology, Orthopaedics) are the primary bottlenecks.

3. RESOURCE & COST EFFICIENCY
   With health expenditure rising, there is a need to correlate spending with 
   performance outcomes across different states and hospital peer groups.


WHAT THIS PLATFORM DOES
------------------------
A centralized analytics engine that processes AIHW (Australian Institute of 
Health and Welfare) national data to provide a "single source of truth" for 
system-wide performance:

  Question                              Where to find the answer
  ------------------------------------  ----------------------------------
  How are ED wait times trending?       Notebook 01, Power BI ED Overview
  Which triage levels are most delayed? Notebook 01, Triage Performance page
  What is the elective backlog size?    Notebook 03, Elective Overview
  Which specialties have longest waits? Notebook 03, Specialty Analysis
  Are some states performing better?    Notebook 01/03, State Comparison
  How is the Star Schema structured?    Notebook 02/04, Data Modelling

KEY FINDINGS (PRELIMINARY)
---------------------------
1. TRIAGE 3 (URGENT) PERFORMANCE VARYING
   While Triage 1 (Resuscitation) targets are largely met, Triage 3 patients 
   often face the greatest variability in "Seen on Time" metrics across states.

2. PEER GROUP CLASSIFICATION MATTERS
   Principal referral hospitals (Group 3) consistently handle higher volumes 
   but show different wait time distributions compared to Public Acute Group A.

3. SPECIALTY BOTTLENECKS
   Orthopaedic and Ophthalmology surgeries consistently report higher median 
   wait times compared to General Surgery across most Australian states.

4. MEDALLION ARCHITECTURE UTILITY
   The shift from complex AIHW Excel sheets to a clean Star Schema reduced 
   Power BI report load times and simplified DAX measure complexity.
